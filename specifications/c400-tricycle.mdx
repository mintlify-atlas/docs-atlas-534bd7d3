# C-400 Electric Tricycle

The C-400 is a robust electric tricycle designed for efficient cargo transport. Its three-wheel design provides superior stability and load-bearing capacity, making it ideal for commercial deliveries and heavy-duty applications.

## Overview

The C-400 offers a perfect balance between range, capacity, and reliability. With its powerful LifePO4 battery system and sturdy construction, this vehicle is engineered for demanding transport needs in urban environments.

<Note>
The three-wheel design provides exceptional stability when carrying heavy loads, reducing the risk of tipping and improving driver confidence.
</Note>

| Specification | Value |
|--------------|-------|
