# C-800 Electric Tricycle

The C-800 is our premium electric tricycle featuring an enclosed cabin that provides weather protection for the driver. With an impressive 800 kg load capacity, it represents the ultimate solution for heavy cargo transport and passenger applications.

## Overview

The C-800 combines the stability of a three-wheel design with the comfort of an enclosed cabin. This vehicle is engineered for operators who need maximum cargo capacity while maintaining driver comfort in all weather conditions.

<Note>
The enclosed cabin significantly improves driver comfort and safety, providing protection from rain, sun, and adverse weather conditions during operations.
</Note>
