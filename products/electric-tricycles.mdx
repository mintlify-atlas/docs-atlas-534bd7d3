| **Range** | Up to 70km |

| **Load Capacity** | Up to 400 kg |

- **High Stability**: Three-wheel design ensures balanced handling even with heavy loads

- **Durable Battery**: LifePO4 technology provides long-lasting, reliable power
- **Generous Capacity**: Transport up to 400kg of cargo efficiently
- **Zero Emissions**: Eco-friendly electric operation reduces environmental impact

## Common Features Across All Models

### LifePO4 Battery Technology

Both tricycle models utilize advanced LifePO4 (Lithium Iron Phosphate) battery systems:

- **Long Lifespan**: Up to 2000+ charge cycles
- **Safe Chemistry**: More stable and safer than traditional lithium-ion
- **Consistent Performance**: Maintains voltage throughout discharge cycle
- **Fast Charging**: Reduced downtime with efficient charging
- **Temperature Tolerance**: Performs well in various climate conditions

### Environmental Benefits

- Zero direct emissions
- Reduced noise pollution
- Lower carbon footprint compared to gasoline vehicles
- Energy-efficient electric motors
- Contribution to cleaner urban air quality

### Cost Efficiency

- Lower operating costs compared to fuel-powered vehicles
- Minimal maintenance requirements
- No fuel costs - charge with standard electricity
- Reduced wear on brake systems due to regenerative braking
- Long-term savings on total cost of ownership
