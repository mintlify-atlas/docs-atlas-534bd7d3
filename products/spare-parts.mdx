## Maintenance Recommendations

### Tire Maintenance

- **Pressure Checks**: Inspect tire pressure weekly
- **Tread Depth**: Monitor tread wear regularly
- **Visual Inspection**: Check for cuts, punctures, or damage
- **Rotation**: Follow recommended rotation schedules
- **Replacement**: Replace when tread depth reaches minimum specification

### General Parts Maintenance

- **Regular Inspections**: Check critical components monthly
- **Preventive Replacement**: Replace wear items before failure
- **Keep Spares**: Maintain inventory of common replacement parts
- **Follow Schedules**: Adhere to manufacturer maintenance intervals
- **Document Service**: Keep records of all part replacements

<Note>
  Regular maintenance with OEM parts significantly extends vehicle lifespan and maintains optimal performance. See our [Maintenance Guide](/guides/maintenance) for detailed schedules.
</Note>
